Referenced vocabularies

SKOS / RDF vocabulary resources dereferenced by IRI from the blocks. The local ontology is also published as the Ontology building block; the canonical long-term target for controlled vocabularies in the OGC ecosystem is OGC Rainbow. Today the local TTL is the source of truth; the rainbowUri slot in vocabularies.json is reserved for the migration target.
